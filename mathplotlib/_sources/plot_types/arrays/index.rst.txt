Gridded data:
-------------

Plots of arrays and images :math:`Z_{i, j}` and fields :math:`U_{i, j}, V_{i, j}`
on `regular grids <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Regular_grid>`_ and
corresponding coordinate grids :math:`X_{i,j}, Y_{i,j}`.
